요인설계(Factorial Design)¶

각각의 완전한 시행이나 실험의 반복에서 요인수준의 모든 가능한 조합을 조사하는 것을 의미한다.

교호작용 유무:

  • 교호작용이 없으면 A와 B가 상관없이 움직여서 효과에 따른 변화가 평행하다.
  • 교호작용이 있으면 A와 B가 상호작용하기 때문에 효과에 따른 변화가 더 역동적으로 변한다.
    • 2요인일 경우에 주효과보다 교호작용이 매우 크면 그래프상으로 반응변수의 추이선이 교차한다.

회귀모형 및 반응표면¶

2요인 요인실험의 회귀모형

$$ y=\beta_0 + \beta_1 x_1 +\beta_2x_2 + \beta_{12} x_1 x_2 +\epsilon $$

교호작용항을 배제한 경우

교호작용항이 포함된 경우

교호작용을 포함시키면 잘못된 결론 도출을 피할 수 있다.

2요인 요인설계¶

A요인의 수준이 a, B요인의 수준이 b, 관측이 n번 반복

완전 랜덤화 설계 (Completely Randomized Design)

효과모형

$$ y_{ijk}= \mu + \tau_i + \beta_j (\tau\beta)_{ij}+\epsilon_{ijk}\begin{cases} i=1,2,...,a\\ j=1,2,...,b\\ k=1,2,...,n \end{cases} $$

$$ \sum_{i=1}^a \tau_i = 0, \sum_{j=1}^b \beta_j = 0, \sum_{j=1}^b\sum_{i=1}^a (\tau\beta)_{ij}=0 $$

$$ H_0 : \tau_1 = \tau_2 = \cdots = \tau_a = 0 \\ H_1: \text{At least one of } \tau_i \neq 0 \text{ is true} $$

$$ H_0 : \beta_1 = \beta_2 = \cdots = \beta_b = 0 \\ H_1: \text{At least one of } \beta_i \neq 0 \text{ is true} $$

$$ H_0 : (\tau\beta)_{ij}=0\ \forall(i,j)\\ H_1: \text{At least one of } (\tau\beta)_{i,j} \neq 0 \text{ is true} $$

Two-way ANOVA (Factorial ANOVA)¶

요인이 2개인 이원분산분석, 교호작용항까지 포함이 가능하다.

  • 고정효과모형

$$ \overline{y}_{i..} = \frac{1}{bn} \sum_{j=1}^{b} \sum_{k=1}^{n} y_{ijk} \quad i = 1, 2, \ldots, a $$

$$ \overline{y}_{.j.} = \frac{1}{an} \sum_{i=1}^{a} \sum_{k=1}^{n} y_{ijk} \quad j = 1, 2, \ldots, b $$

$$ \sum_{i=1}^{a} \sum_{j=1}^{b} \sum_{k=1}^{n} \left( y_{ijk} - \overline{y} \right)^2 = bn \sum_{i=1}^{a} \left( \overline{y}_{i..} - \overline{y} \right)^2 + an \sum_{j=1}^{b} \left( \overline{y}_{.j.} - \overline{y} \right)^2 + \\ n \sum_{i=1}^{a} \sum_{j=1}^{b} \left( \overline{y}_{ij.} - \overline{y}_{i..} - \overline{y}_{.j.} + \overline{y} \right)^2 + \sum_{i=1}^{a} \sum_{j=1}^{b} \sum_{k=1}^{n} \left( y_{ijk} - \overline{y}_{ij.} \right)^2 $$

$$ SS_T = SS_A + SS_B + SS_{AB} + SS_E $$

$$ df \text{ breakdown:}\\ \quad abn - 1 = (a - 1) + (b - 1) + (a - 1)(b - 1) + ab(n - 1) $$

$$ SS_T = SS_A + SS_B + SS_{AB} + SS_E $$

$$ SS_T = \sum_{i=1}^{a} \sum_{j=1}^{b} \sum_{k=1}^{n} y_{ijk}^2 - \frac{y_{...}^2}{abn} $$

$$ SS_A = \frac{1}{bn} \sum_{i=1}^{a} y_{i..}^2 - \frac{y_{...}^2}{abn} $$

$$ SS_B = \frac{1}{an} \sum_{j=1}^{b} y_{.j.}^2 - \frac{y_{...}^2}{abn} $$

$$ SS_{\text{Subtotals}} = \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{a} \sum_{j=1}^{b} y_{ij.}^2 - \frac{y_{...}^2}{abn} $$

$$ SS_{AB} = SS_{\text{Subtotals}} - SS_A - SS_B $$

$$ SS_E = SS_T - SS_{AB} - SS_A - SS_B $$

Source of Variation Sum of Squares Degrees of Freedom Mean Square $ F_0 $
$ A $ treatments $ SS_A $ $ a - 1 $ $ MS_A = \frac{SS_A}{a - 1} $ $ F_0 = \frac{MS_A}{MS_E} $
$ B $ treatments $ SS_B $ $ b - 1 $ $ MS_B = \frac{SS_B}{b - 1} $ $ F_0 = \frac{MS_B}{MS_E} $
Interaction $ SS_{AB} $ $ (a - 1)(b - 1) $ $ MS_{AB} = \frac{SS_{AB}}{(a - 1)(b - 1)} $ $ F_0 = \frac{MS_{AB}}{MS_E} $
Error $ SS_E $ $ ab(n - 1) $ $ MS_E = \frac{SS_E}{ab(n - 1)} $
Total $ SS_T $ $ abn - 1 $

3요인 요인 설계 (Three-Factor Factorial Design)¶

개요: 세 개의 독립 요인을 다루며, 요인 A, B, C 각각의 주효과 및 교호작용 효과를 분석한다.

모형 식: $$ Y_{ijkl} = \mu + \tau_i + \beta_j + \gamma_k + (\tau\beta)_{ij} + (\tau\gamma)_{ik} + (\beta\gamma)_{jk} + \\ (\tau\beta\gamma)_{ijk} + \epsilon_{ijkl}\begin{cases} i = 1,2,...a\\ j=1,2,...,b\\ k=1,2,...,c\\ l=1,2,...,n \end{cases} $$

변동원인 제곱합 ($SS$) 자유도 ($df$) 평균제곱 ($MS$) 기대평균제곱 $F_0$
A $SS_A$ $a - 1$ $MS_A$ $\sigma^2 + \frac{bcn}{a - 1} \sum \tau_i^2$ $F_0 = \frac{MS_A}{MS_E}$
B $SS_B$ $b - 1$ $MS_B$ $\sigma^2 + \frac{acn}{b - 1} \sum \beta_j^2$ $F_0 = \frac{MS_B}{MS_E}$
C $SS_C$ $c - 1$ $MS_C$ $\sigma^2 + \frac{abn}{c - 1} \sum \gamma_k^2$ $F_0 = \frac{MS_C}{MS_E}$
AB $SS_{AB}$ $(a - 1)(b - 1)$ $MS_{AB}$ $\sigma^2 + \frac{cn}{(a - 1)(b - 1)} \sum (\tau \beta)_{ij}^2$ $F_0 = \frac{MS_{AB}}{MS_E}$
AC $SS_{AC}$ $(a - 1)(c - 1)$ $MS_{AC}$ $\sigma^2 + \frac{bn}{(a - 1)(c - 1)} \sum (\tau \gamma)_{ik}^2$ $F_0 = \frac{MS_{AC}}{MS_E}$
BC $SS_{BC}$ $(b - 1)(c - 1)$ $MS_{BC}$ $\sigma^2 + \frac{an}{(b - 1)(c - 1)} \sum (\beta \gamma)_{jk}^2$ $F_0 = \frac{MS_{BC}}{MS_E}$
ABC $SS_{ABC}$ $(a - 1)(b - 1)(c - 1)$ $MS_{ABC}$ $\sigma^2 + \frac{n}{(a - 1)(b - 1)(c - 1)} \sum (\tau \beta \gamma)_{ijk}^2$ $F_0 = \frac{MS_{ABC}}{MS_E}$
오차 $SS_E$ $abc(n - 1)$ $MS_E$ $\sigma^2$ -
합계 $SS_T$ $abcn - 1$ - - -

기대평균제곱: 오차에 의한 변동과 변인에 의한 변동을 이론적으로 분리한 것

$$ SS_T = \sum_{i=1}^{a} \sum_{j=1}^{b} \sum_{k=1}^{c} \sum_{l=1}^{n} y_{ijkl}^2 - \frac{y_{....}^2}{abcn} $$

$$ SS_A = \frac{1}{bcn} \sum_{i=1}^{a} y_{i\cdot\cdot\cdot}^2 - \frac{y_{....}^2}{abcn} $$

$$ SS_B = \frac{1}{acn} \sum_{j=1}^{b} y_{\cdot j\cdot\cdot}^2 - \frac{y_{....}^2}{abcn} $$

$$ SS_C = \frac{1}{abn} \sum_{k=1}^{c} y_{\cdot\cdot k\cdot}^2 - \frac{y_{....}^2}{abcn} $$

$$ SS_{AB} = \frac{1}{cn} \sum_{i=1}^{a} \sum_{j=1}^{b} y_{ij\cdot\cdot}^2 - \frac{y_{\cdot\cdot\cdot\cdot}^2}{abcn} - SS_A - SS_B =\\ SS_{\text{Subtotals}(AB)} - SS_A - SS_B $$

$$ SS_{AC} = \frac{1}{bn} \sum_{i=1}^{a} \sum_{k=1}^{c} y_{i\cdot k\cdot}^2 - \frac{y_{\cdot\cdot\cdot\cdot}^2}{abcn} - SS_A - SS_C =\\ SS_{\text{Subtotals}(AC)} - SS_A - SS_C $$

$$ SS_{BC} = \frac{1}{an} \sum_{j=1}^{b} \sum_{k=1}^{c} y_{\cdot jk\cdot}^2 - \frac{y_{\cdot\cdot\cdot\cdot}^2}{abcn} - SS_B - SS_C =\\ SS_{\text{Subtotals}(BC)} - SS_B - SS_C $$

$$ SS_{ABC} =\\ \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{a} \sum_{j=1}^{b} \sum_{k=1}^{c} y_{ijk\cdot}^2 - \frac{y_{\cdot\cdot\cdot\cdot}^2}{abcn} - \\SS_A - SS_B - SS_C - SS_{AB} - SS_{AC} - SS_{BC} = \\SS_{\text{Subtotals}(ABC)} - SS_A - SS_B - SS_C - SS_{AB} - SS_{AC} - SS_{BC} $$

$$ SS_E = SS_T - SS_{\text{Subtotals}(ABC)} $$

2요인 랜덤화 완전블록 요인설계¶

$$ y_{ijk} = \mu + \tau_i + \beta_j + (\tau\beta)_{ij} + \delta_k + \epsilon_{ijk} \quad \begin{cases} i = 1, 2, \ldots, a \\ j = 1, 2, \ldots, b \\ k = 1, 2, \ldots, n \\ \end{cases} $$

변동원인 제곱합 자유도 평균제곱 $ F_0 $
블록 $ \frac{1}{ab} \sum_{k=1}^n y_{..k}^2 - \frac{y_{...}^2}{abn} $ $ n - 1 $ $\sigma^2 + ab\sigma^2_{\delta}$
A $ \frac{1}{bn} \sum_{i=1}^a y_{i..}^2 - \frac{y_{...}^2}{abn} $ $ a - 1 $ $ MS_A $ $ \frac{MS_A}{MS_E} $
B $ \frac{1}{an} \sum_{j=1}^b y_{.j.}^2 - \frac{y_{...}^2}{abn} $ $ b - 1 $ $ MS_B $ $ \frac{MS_B}{MS_E} $
AB $ \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^a \sum_{j=1}^b y_{ij.}^2 - SS_A - SS_B - \frac{y_{...}^2}{abn} $ $ (a-1)(b-1) $ $ MS_{AB} $ $ \frac{MS_{AB}}{MS_E} $
오차 차감 $ (ab-1)(n-1) $ $ MS_E $ -
합계 $ \sum_{i=1}^a \sum_{j=1}^b \sum_{k=1}^n y_{ijk}^2 - \frac{y_{...}^2}{abn} $ $ abn - 1 $ - -

여기서:

  • $ MS_A = \frac{SS_A}{a-1} $
  • $ MS_B = \frac{SS_B}{b-1} $
  • $ MS_{AB} = \frac{SS_{AB}}{(a-1)(b-1)} $
  • $ MS_E = \frac{SS_E}{(ab-1)(n-1)} $
